# Modular Golems

A datapack/mod that allows you to create iron golems from a variety of materials, each with unique properties and abilities. Customize your golems with powerful upgrades to suit your needs!

## Overview

Modular Golems expands on the vanilla Iron Golem by introducing multiple material types and an upgrade system. Build golems using different blocks and unlock special abilities through upgrades.

### Features

- **11 Golem Materials** - Craft golems from Copper, Gold, Diamond, Emerald, Netherite, and more
- **Unique Abilities** - Each material grants different stats and special effects
- **Upgrade System** - Enhance your golems with various upgrades
- **Resource Pack** - Visual indicators to easily identify different golem types

## How to Build a Golem

Similar to vanilla Iron Golems, place 4 material blocks in a T-shape and top with a carved pumpkin. The material blocks determine the golem type and its abilities.

### Construction Pattern

```
      [Pumpkin]
[Block][Block][Block]
       [Block]
```
