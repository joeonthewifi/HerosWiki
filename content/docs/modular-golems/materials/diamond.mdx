## Special Abilities

### Diamond Durability

- Increased resistance to all damage types
- Natural armor toughness
- Longer lifespan than lower-tier golems

### Efficient Damage

- Consistent high damage output
- Effective against most mob types
- Balanced attack speed

## Best Used For

- Late-game base defense
- Boss fight assistance
- High-value area protection
- Long-term investments

## Upgrades

Works exceptionally well with:

- **Regeneration Upgrade** - Makes it nearly unkillable
- **Thorns Upgrade** - Reflects damage to attackers
- **Knockback Resistance** - Prevents displacement
- **Fire Aspect** - Adds burn damage

## Comparison

### vs Netherite Golem

- Slightly less health and damage
- Faster movement speed
- More cost-effective (no ancient debris needed)

### vs Emerald Golem

- Higher combat stats
- Less utility (no villager bonuses)
- Better for pure defense

## Tips

- Expensive but worth the investment for important areas
- Combine with regeneration for near-immortality
- Excellent balance makes it adaptable to any situation
- Consider using multiple for large perimeters
