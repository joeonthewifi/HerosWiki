## Special Abilities

### Oxidation

- Copper Golems oxidize over time like copper blocks
- Changes appearance through stages: Fresh → Exposed → Weathered → Oxidized
- Can be waxed to prevent oxidation
- Can be scraped to reverse oxidation

### Lightning Affinity

- Takes reduced damage from lightning
- May gain temporary power boost when struck

## Best Used For

- Early game defense
- Decorative purposes (various oxidation states)
- Lightning rod protection
- Budget-friendly option

## Upgrades

Compatible with all standard upgrades. Consider:

- **Speed Upgrade** - Compensates for medium base speed
- **Health Upgrade** - Increases survivability
- **Damage Upgrade** - Boosts low base attack

## Tips

- Wax your Copper Golem to maintain its appearance
- Place near lightning rods for potential power boosts
- Cost-effective for multiple golem setups
- Great starter golem before gathering rare materials
