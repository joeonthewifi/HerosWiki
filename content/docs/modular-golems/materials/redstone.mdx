## Special Abilities

- **Redstone Interaction**: Can trigger pressure plates, buttons
- **Signal Boost**: Strengthens nearby redstone signals
- **Automatic Defense**: Activates defensive contraptions when attacked
- **Power Detection**: Senses nearby powered blocks

## Best For

- Redstone farms
- Automated defense systems
- Technical builds
- Tripwire/pressure plate activation
