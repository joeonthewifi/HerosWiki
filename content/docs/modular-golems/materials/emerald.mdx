# <img src="https://minecraft.wiki/images/Invicon_Block_of_Emerald.png" alt="Emerald Golem" className="size-6" /> Emerald Golem

A golem designed for village protection with special bonuses around villagers and trading.

## Stats

- Difference vs Iron Golem base
  - Speed: +0.03 (0.28)
  - Knockback Resistance: +0.00 (1.00)
  - Health: +80 (180)
  - Step Height: +0.00 (1)
  - Armor: +8 (8)
  - Armor Toughness: +0 (0)

## Special Abilities

- **Villager Bond**: Increased effectiveness near villagers
- **Trading Boost**: May improve nearby villager trades
- **Raid Master**: Bonus damage against raid mobs
- **Hero of the Village**: Synergizes with the effect

## Best For

- Trading halls
- Village defense
- Raid protection
- Iron farms (doesn't scare villagers)
