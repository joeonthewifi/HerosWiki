The ultimate defensive golem with maximum health, fire immunity, and knockback resistance. The strongest single-target protector available.

## Crafting

Build using 4 Netherite Blocks in a T-shape topped with a Carved Pumpkin.

### Required Materials

- 4x Netherite Block (36 netherite ingots total) <span className="mc-item-count"><img src="https://minecraft.wiki/images/Invicon_Block_of_Netherite.png" alt="" className="h-7 w-7" /><span className="count">4</span></span>
- 1x Carved Pumpkin <img src="https://minecraft.wiki/images/Invicon_Carved_Pumpkin.png" alt="" className="h-7 w-7" />

**Cost Breakdown**: 36 netherite ingots = 36 ancient debris + 36 gold ingots + 144 netherite scrap

## Special Abilities

### Fire Immunity

- Completely immune to fire and lava damage
- Can fight blazes and wither without taking fire damage
- Perfect for Nether defense

### Knockback Resistance

- Cannot be knocked back by attacks
- Holds position in combat
- Effective against high-knockback mobs

### Maximum Durability

- Highest health pool of any golem
- Natural damage resistance
- Extremely long lifespan

### Nether Affinity

- Increased effectiveness in the Nether
- Bonus damage against Nether mobs
- Can operate in lava

## Best Used For

- Wither boss fights
- Nether fortress/bastion defense
- Protecting against flying mobs
- Ultimate base protection
- Lava lake guardians

## Upgrades

Recommended enhancements:

- **Regeneration** - Makes it virtually unkillable
- **Thorns III** - Massive damage reflection
- **Speed Upgrade** - Compensates for slow base speed
- **Strength** - Push damage to extreme levels

## Comparison

### vs Diamond Golem

- 50 more HP

- Fire immunity and knockback resistance
- Significantly more expensive
- Slower movement

### vs Super Golem

- More specialized for defense
- Lower cost than Super
- Missing Super's utility abilities
