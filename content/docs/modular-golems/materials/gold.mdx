# <img src="https://minecraft.wiki/images/Invicon_Block_of_Gold.png" alt="Gold Golem" className="size-6" /> Gold Golem

A high-speed golem that sacrifices durability for incredible movement and attack speed.

## Stats

- Difference vs Iron Golem base
  - Speed: +0.10 (0.35)
  - Knockback Resistance: +0.00 (1.00)
  - Health: -20 (80)
  - Step Height: +0.00 (1)
  - Armor: +0 (0)
  - Armor Toughness: +0 (0)

## Special Abilities

- **Speed Boost**: Fastest movement speed of any golem
- **Quick Strikes**: Rapid attack rate
- **Piglin Passive**: Piglins won't attack this golem

## Best For

- Quick response defense
- Hit-and-run tactics
- Chasing fast mobs
- Gold farm areas (piglins won't aggro)
